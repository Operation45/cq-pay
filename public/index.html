What is Civic Quarterly?

Civic Quarterly is a community of people interested in shaping the future of government. Civic Quarterly will publish a quarterly journal in which designers and technologists are encouraged to share stories on the current uses and future applications of civic technology. We've got a lot more in mind, too. We're just getting started.
